Data Cleaning Notes – Digital Banking Adoption Project
Dataset: Global Findex Database (World Bank) – 2021
Source: https://databankfiles.worldbank.org/public/ddpext_download/FINDEX/Databank_WorldDevelopmentIndicators/WDIGlobalFindex2021.xlsx

Cleaning Steps:

Filtered dataset to only include data for the year 2021.

Selected relevant columns related to digital banking and payment adoption indicators.

Renamed columns for clarity and easy analysis:

Country Name → Country

Year → Year

Adult Population → Adult Population

Income Group → Income Group

Other columns were renamed to clearly indicate account ownership, mobile usage, digital payments, etc.

Saved cleaned dataset as digital_banking_adoption_2021.csv.

Next Steps:
Use this cleaned dataset for further analysis and Tableau dashboard creation.
